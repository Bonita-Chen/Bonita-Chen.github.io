## Overview

Scaled AR and credit dashboards from U.S. coverage to EMEA, connecting invoice-level aging to customer-level reporting. The project was completed during a summer internship at Bio-Techne.

## Key Contributions

- Extended existing U.S.-focused Power BI dashboards to cover EMEA markets
- Connected invoice-level aging data to customer-level credit reporting
- Built DAX measures for multi-currency conversion and regional KPIs
- Delivered dashboards used by finance teams for daily accounts receivable monitoring
